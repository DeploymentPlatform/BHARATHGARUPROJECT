Project Scale & Capability

Solar Solutions Portfolio

Engineered Solar Systems

Choose the configuration that best fits your consumption curve and grid stability.

Rooftop Solar Systems

Installed on concrete slabs, metal sheds, or tiled roofs. Makes use of premium monocrystalline half-cut cells to deliver high yields in low-light environments.

Ground-Mounted Plants

Designed for open plots, warehouses, and factories. Built on hot-dip galvanized mounting structures to withstand severe wind speeds.

Hybrid Solar Solutions

Combines solar PV panels, grid power, and battery storage. Continuously switches power sources dynamically to maintain complete blackout immunity.

Net Metering Solutions

Bi-directional grid metering systems. Automatically exports surplus solar energy to the state grid, accumulating credits to offset grid purchases.

Battery Integrated Solar

High-capacity energy storage integration. Stores daytime solar output to power heavy industrial drives or office HVAC systems during night cycles.

Performance Optimization

Retrofitting existing solar setups with high-efficiency micro-inverters, optimized cleaning arrays, and smart digital generation monitors.

Our Method

Solar EPC Lifecycle

We manage the complete project cycle from site inspection to net-meter commissioning.

01

Consultation

Understand customer goals, evaluate historical utility bills, and outline return-on-investment parameters.

02

Site Survey

Examine roof structural strength, execute 3D shadow mapping, and analyze current transformer capacities.

03

Feasibility Analysis

Perform electrical load profile reviews to determine optimized kW sizing and evaluate grid interface requirements.

04

System Design

3D layout generation, cable routing diagrams, breaker calculations, and mechanical wind load structural designing.

05

Procurement

Procuring tier-1 solar panels, high-efficiency grid-tie inverters, DC/AC cables, and safety earthing hardware.

06

Installation

Mounting structural assembly, panels positioning, electrical wiring integration, and lightning protection setups.

07

Commissioning

Pre-commissioning safety checks, net-metering synchronization, grid charging, and utility inspector certifications.

08

Monitoring & Support

Deploy remote digital monitoring platforms, schedule periodic maintenance washes, and verify output yields.
