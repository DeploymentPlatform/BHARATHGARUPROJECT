Bharat Marella

Growth Catalyst | Serial Entrepreneur | Business Strategist

Bharat Marella is a highly accomplished visionary entrepreneur, business strategist, and global growth leader with over 24 years of multi-industry experience across India, Europe, and the Middle East. His career spans engineering, infrastructure execution, corporate transformation, and financial advisory services.

An alumnus of premier management institutions, Bharat completed Executive Management Programs from the **Indian Institute of Management Bangalore (IIM Bangalore)** and the **Indian School of Business (ISB)**. He also holds a B.Tech in Chemical Engineering from Andhra University and is a certified Six Sigma Black Belt practitioner, bringing rigorous process design and operational execution methodologies to all ventures.

As a Founding Board Member of the **BRICS Alliance Business Forum**, Bharat facilitates high-value international trade dialogues, strategic business collaborations, and cross-border partnerships across BRICS nations and global target markets. He has built an impressive track record in startup ecosystems, successfully scaling five startups from ground zero to multi-million valuations.

At Trinity Power Solutions, Bharat acts as the Business Catalyst and Strategic Advisor. He shapes TPS's long-term corporate governance, strategic branding direction, institutional client relationships, and technological alliances, ensuring the company delivers sustainable, premium energy infrastructure.
