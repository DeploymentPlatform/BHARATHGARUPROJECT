Industries We Serve

Providing robust, customized power solutions for every sector.

Industrial Plants & Manufacturing

Heavy machineries, CNC cutters, and automatic assembly lines are highly susceptible to voltage fluctuations and power sags. We design heavy-duty Servo stabilizers and distribution transformers to secure continuous runtime.

Core Solutions: Voltage Stabilization, Substation Transformers, APFC Panels, AMC

Commercial Complexes & Malls

High-occupancy spaces require balanced, highly optimized power distribution to feed central HVAC chillers, elevators, lighting, and retail outlets safely. Our APFC panels keep power factor optimal, eliminating reactive penalties.

Core Solutions: LT Panels, APFC Panels, Energy Audits, Rooftop Solar EPC

Residential Communities & Townships

Modern gated communities require unified backup layouts for common lighting, backup generator changeovers, and common pumping networks. We install reliable AMF panels and common area hybrid solar infrastructure.

Core Solutions: AMF Panels, Home Inverters & Batteries, Common-Area Solar EPC

Hospitals & Healthcare Facilities

Medical diagnostics, MRI/CT scanners, ICU ventilators, and operation theaters require clean, noise-free electricity. A 10-millisecond blackout can corrupt patient scans and place lives at risk. We install ultra-fast static stabilizers and isolation systems.

Core Solutions: Static Stabilizers, Isolation Transformers, Online UPS, 24/7 AMC Support

Educational Institutions

Universities, science laboratories, and high-enrollment schools need stable power networks to protect delicate computer labs and research instruments. Large rooftops make institutions optimal fits for solar offset.

Core Solutions: Rooftop Solar EPC, Distribution Panels, Online UPS, Site AMC

IT Offices & Data-Sensitive Hubs

Server clusters, cloud data hubs, and high-density office computers require complete isolation from incoming utility harmonic distortions and zero-millisecond power transfer during outages. We deploy high-capacity Online UPS modules.

Core Solutions: Online UPS, Harmonic Mitigation, Power Distribution Units (PDU)

Retail Showrooms & Supermarkets

Continuous lighting, cold-storage refrigeration, and checkout billing systems require seamless changeover backups. We supply commercial stabilizer arrays to prevent damage to cooling compressors.

Core Solutions: Servo Stabilizers, Inverters, PCC Panels, Maintenance Services

Infrastructure & Substation Projects

Large airport developments, logistics parks, and railway yards require high-capacity substations. We provide engineering and procurement services for transformers up to 3 MVA and automated feeder grids.

Core Solutions: 300 kVA - 3 MVA Transformers, PCC Panels, Turnkey Commissioning

Villas & Premium Townhouses

High-end residences run home theaters, premium kitchens, and heating. We implement decentralized solar systems combined with customized domestic voltage protection boards.

Core Solutions: Net-Metered Rooftop Solar, Hybrid Inverters, Voltage Protectors

SMEs & Commercial Offices

Small-to-medium businesses require cost-effective backup power configurations to run routers, printers, and core machines, preventing workplace productivity interruptions.

Core Solutions: Commercial Inverters, Double-Conversion UPS, Electrical Panels
