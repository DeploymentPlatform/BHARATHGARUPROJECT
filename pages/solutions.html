Systems Protection

Power Conditioning Equipment

Servo Voltage Stabilizers

Motor-driven buck-boost stabilizers designed to correct wide fluctuations in industrial plants and commercial complexes. Features micro-controlled logic with high overload capacities.

  • High efficiency (>98.5%)
  • Digital voltage & current meters
  • Under/over voltage trip protections
  • For manufacturing, hospitals, CNC machinery

Static Voltage Stabilizers

Solid-state stabilizers operating without moving mechanical parts. Utilizes IGBT/Thyristor controls to rectify voltage fluctuations within milliseconds, protecting highly sensitive equipment.

  • Instantaneous correction speed (20-30 ms)
  • Silent, maintenance-free operations
  • Compact footprint
  • Ideal for IT servers, medical scans, lasers

Isolation Transformers

Ultra-shielded isolation transformers designed to decouple primary and secondary electrical networks, effectively suppressing line noises, spikes, and high-frequency harmonics.

  • Excellent noise attenuation (120 dB)
  • Dry-type/oil-cooled variants
  • Electrostatic shielding grid
  • Ideal for telecom, data rooms, testing labs

Power Distribution Units (PDU)

Designed for data centers and critical server rooms. TPS engineers modular PDUs to distribute conditioned power to individual racks while tracking real-time load analytics.

  • Individual circuit breaker monitors
  • Isolated grounding layouts
  • Integrated transient surge suppression
  • Real-time RS-485 network reports
Efficiency Optimization

Power Quality & Network Efficiency

Heavy-Duty Power Capacitors

Industrial-grade power factor improvement capacitors engineered to decrease inductive currents in networks, thereby lowering energy bills and transformer thermal loading.

APFC Panels

Automatic Power Factor Correction panels. Incorporates intelligent controllers that automatically switch capacitor banks in steps to maintain power factor close to unity (0.99).

  • Saves reactive energy surcharges
  • Prevents over-compensation and spikes
  • Microprocessor-based step selection
  • For commercial malls, packaging plants, offices

Harmonic Mitigation Systems

Active and passive harmonic filters designed to eliminate non-linear current distortion from IT servers, VFD drives, and LED loads. Protects cables from overheating.

  • Reduces Total Harmonic Distortion (THD)
  • Compliance with IEEE 519 standards
  • Dynamic compensation response
  • Improves transformer lifespan

Electrical Distribution Infrastructure

LT (Low Tension) Panels

Robust metal-enclosed distribution boards designed for systems up to 1000V AC. Fabricated in-house using premium switchgear, ensuring high short-circuit withstand thresholds.

PCC (Power Control Center)

Main power distribution hub boards containing air circuit breakers (ACB) and molded case circuit breakers (MCCB). Engineers safe control over entire facility sub-grids.

AMF (Auto Main Failure) Panels

Automates startup and shutdown operations of diesel generators when utility grids drop. Seamlessly transitions critical emergency feeders without manual inputs.

  • Automatic transfer logic control
  • Battery chargers integrated
  • Generator monitoring displays
  • Protective interlocks built in

Backup Power Systems

Online UPS Systems
Zero-Transition Protection

An Online Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) continuously filters input utility electricity and converts it through a double-conversion process (AC-DC-AC) before feeding the load. When the grid fails, the inverter runs directly off the batteries with **zero milliseconds** transfer time.

  • Transfer Time 0 milliseconds (Instantaneous)
  • Voltage Regulation Constant output (±1% stability)
  • Target Load Computers, IT servers, medical machinery
  • Protection Scope Protects from spikes, surges, sags, drops
  • Ideal For Data-sensitive and industrial automation grids
Home Inverters
Delayed Changeover Backup

An inverter passes raw grid electricity to connected appliances during healthy cycles. When a blackout occurs, an internal relay transfers the load path to battery reserves. This transfer involves a short mechanical delay of **10 to 20 milliseconds**.

  • Transfer Time 10 - 20 milliseconds delay
  • Voltage Regulation Grid dependent (limited stability)
  • Target Load Lights, ceiling fans, televisions, appliances
  • Protection Scope Protects from basic utility blackouts
  • Ideal For Residential apartments and standard commercial lights
